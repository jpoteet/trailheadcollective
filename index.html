About Us

Adveture begins at the trailhead

And we believe great adventures deserve great apparel.

Tri-Blend Shirts

I've been in the apparel industry for more than 5 years and have since become a t-shirt snob. The tri-blend I use is from Bella Canvas. Trust me, it will be your new favorite t-shirt.

Unique Designs

I may not print every shirt myself, but the designs are my own creations. I get inspired when I'm on my own adventures in the beautiful outdoors of Colorado.

Professional Prints

Each shirt is printed with DTG, which stands for direct-to-garment. Don't worry, it's high quality and won't compromise the comfort of your new favorite shirt.

Low Prices

This isn't my get rich quick scheme. I've set my prices to be just a few bucks above my cost. I just want you to have an awesome shirt!
